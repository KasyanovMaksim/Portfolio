MY BACKGROUND

I started in front-end engineering, working with Angular architectures and design systems, and gradually moved toward product-level problem-solving. This technical foundation shaped my perspective as a Product Designer: I see interfaces as systems—driven by logic, user roles, and scalable structure.

My engineering background helps me translate product goals into clear flows, consistent patterns, and solutions that balance user needs with technical constraints. I focus on ecosystems rather than isolated screens, ensuring long-term coherence and maintainability.

Today, I work at the intersection of Product Design and engineering, creating token-based design systems, modular UI patterns, and role-driven flows that bring clarity and consistency across teams. My strength is organizing complexity and shaping scalable design foundations that support accessible, cohesive user experiences.
